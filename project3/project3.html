About Me

Hi! I'm Kassandra Viason, and my journey into Computer Science began during my senior year of high school when I enrolled in a computer programming class, where I gained a basic understanding on how to code. That initial exposure has made me very interested to learn more about the tech field. I find the process of coding enjoyable and I am motivated to become an expert and explore job opportunities within the tech industry.

Aside from coding, I am also passionate about art. My aim is to learn more about music production, 3d modelling, and photo/video editing. These creative outlets allow me to bring my ideas to life and express myself in unique ways. Also, I spend quality time with my loved ones and enjoy going on walks or bike rides by myself while listening to music to clear my mind.

Education

I'm currently a full-time student at Queensborough Community College (QCC), working towards an Associate of Science in Computer Science, with an expected completion date in 2025. As a member of the Accelerated Study in Associate Programs (ASAP), I'm committed to maximizing my academic growth and success. In addition, I'm a member of QCC's Coding Club, seizing the opportunity to explore further into the programming world. Previously, I graduated from Valley Stream Central High School in 2023. My academic aspirations include maintaining a strong GPA, earning an Associate in Science degree (AS) in Computer Science, and transferring to a four-year college to pursue my Bachelor's degree.

Work Experience

I have previous experience working in a fast-paced customer food service environment, where I effectively applied my skills in teamwork, time management, adaptability, and professionalism. This role allowed me to develop my communication skills within a dynamic work setting, including managing the cash register and accommodating customers. My diverse responsibilities included food preparation, dishwashing, cooking, cleaning, and maintaining hygiene standards. The skills that I gained from working have helped me navigate new challenges and seek opportunities, which applies to both my personal and professional endeavors.

Communication

During my time working, I've noticed a significant shift in how I perceive interactions with customers and coworkers due to my introverted nature. Engaging in constant conversation has gradually diminished some of my shyness, leading me to worry less about how others perceive me when I communicate.

Teamwork

I acquired valuable teamwork skills, mastering the art of collaboration and using my problem solving abilities. Engaging in collective efforts not only fostered productivity but also instilled in me the importance of seeking assistance when necessary.

Time Management

I mastered the skill of time management by ensuring punctuality and never being late for work. This commitment to timeliness extended beyond the workplace, as I prioritize respecting others' time in all aspects of my life. Now, I effectively manage my time between school commitments, consistently arriving early and maintaining a diligent approach to completing assignments. This skill remains important in my everyday life.
