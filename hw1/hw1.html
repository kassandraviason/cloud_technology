What is so special about these four museums?

The Louvre in Paris boasts a vast art collection, notably featuring the iconic Mona Lisa. Transitioning to Asia, the National Museum of China unfolds the layers of China's cultural history. Across the globe, in Vatican City, the Vatican Museums house impressive art and historical treasures. Finally, The Met stands as a world-class institution, offering diverse exhibits spanning centuries and cultures.

What is the main purpose of museums?

The purpose of modern museums is to collect, preserve, interpret, and display objects of artistic, cultural, or scientific significance for the study and education of the public.
